How this works

This is a resident-maintained issue register for Wicks Place. One central place where owners and residents can log issues, see what others have raised, and understand how widely a problem is felt.

It exists because building management works better with clear, direct feedback from the people who live here. This backlog gives our building manager and strata committee a prioritised, living list of what matters to residents, and it's updated in real time.

When you upvote an issue, you're adding weight to it. The more upvotes an issue has, the easier it is to see its level of importance, and have action prioritised for it accordingly.

If you have any thoughts or suggestions about the backlog, please share your feedback.
